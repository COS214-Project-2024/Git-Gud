\doxysection*{🌆 City Builder 🏙️ }



 

  

This project is part of the COS 214 course and aims to develop a dynamic simulation of urban growth, resource management, and citizen satisfaction using C++. 🚧\hypertarget{md__2_2wsl_8localhost_2_ubuntu_2home_2zandervanschoor_2_university_2_year_012_2_c_o_s_01214_2_git_hub_2_git-_gud_2_r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md0}{}\doxysubsection{\texorpdfstring{📝 Project Overview}{📝 Project Overview}}\label{md__2_2wsl_8localhost_2_ubuntu_2home_2zandervanschoor_2_university_2_year_012_2_c_o_s_01214_2_git_hub_2_git-_gud_2_r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md0}
Urbanization is at the core of human civilization, and managing the growth and sustainability of cities requires balancing infrastructure, resources, and citizen needs. In this project, we’ve created a City Builder Simulation where players can design, construct, and manage a thriving city.

The system focuses on\+:


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item {\bfseries{\doxylink{class_building}{Building} and managing urban infrastructure}} (residential, commercial, industrial, and landmarks).
\item {\bfseries{Allocating resources}} like power, water, and waste management.
\item {\bfseries{Managing citizen satisfaction}} based on taxes, amenities, and services.
\item {\bfseries{Dynamic interactions}} between buildings, utilities, citizens, and government.
\end{DoxyItemize}\hypertarget{md__2_2wsl_8localhost_2_ubuntu_2home_2zandervanschoor_2_university_2_year_012_2_c_o_s_01214_2_git_hub_2_git-_gud_2_r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md1}{}\doxysubsubsection{\texorpdfstring{🔑 Features}{🔑 Features}}\label{md__2_2wsl_8localhost_2_ubuntu_2home_2zandervanschoor_2_university_2_year_012_2_c_o_s_01214_2_git_hub_2_git-_gud_2_r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md1}

\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item 🏗️ {\bfseries{Construct Buildings}}\+: Create and manage various types of buildings, each with its own impact on the city.
\item 🚦 {\bfseries{Manage \doxylink{class_transportation}{Transportation}}}\+: Build roads, public transport systems, and manage traffic flow.
\item ⚡ {\bfseries{Resource Management}}\+: Provide utilities like electricity and water to your city and handle shortages or surpluses.
\item 👥 {\bfseries{\doxylink{class_citizen}{Citizen} Simulation}}\+: Model population growth, employment, and satisfaction based on policies.
\item 📊 {\bfseries{Taxation and Governance}}\+: Set and adjust taxes, allocate city budgets, and implement policies to keep citizens happy and the city running smoothly.
\end{DoxyItemize}\hypertarget{md__2_2wsl_8localhost_2_ubuntu_2home_2zandervanschoor_2_university_2_year_012_2_c_o_s_01214_2_git_hub_2_git-_gud_2_r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md2}{}\doxysubsubsection{\texorpdfstring{📅 Important Dates}{📅 Important Dates}}\label{md__2_2wsl_8localhost_2_ubuntu_2home_2zandervanschoor_2_university_2_year_012_2_c_o_s_01214_2_git_hub_2_git-_gud_2_r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md2}
